Our Story

Helping Small Businesses
Shine Online

DesignedByEvo was founded out of a passion for helping small businesses succeed in a competitive digital landscape. We recognised the challenges many companies face when building a professional online presence — and decided to do something about it.

We build high-quality website templates that can be quickly customized for each client — saving time while delivering professional results.

Our goal is simple: make a strong online presence accessible and efficient, delivering scalable, easy-to-manage websites that support long-term growth.

  • Fully customisable templates built around your brand
  • Complete client ownership — your site, your terms
  • Scalable architecture ready to grow with your business
  • Clean, professional design focused on conversion
