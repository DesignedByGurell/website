YOUR SAFETY IS OUR BUSINESS

Founded in 2009, SwiftShield Security has grown from a two-man installation team into the region's most trusted security provider. We believe everyone deserves to feel safe — at home, at work, and everywhere in between.

Every technician on our team is PSIRA-certified, background-checked, and trained to the highest industry standard. We don't outsource, we don't cut corners, and we stand behind every system we install with an industry-leading 3-year workmanship guarantee.
